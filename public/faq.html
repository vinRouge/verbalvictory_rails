1. What should I do before reading FAQ?

Come to our meeting! ;) We meet weekly on Mondays, 7 p.m. in Wyższa Szkoła Bankowa.

And check our Facebook profile for venue updates.

2. Can I come to the meeting if I'm not a member yet?

Of course, we love guests! The more the merrier :) Our guests can come 4 times before they join the club.

3. How can I become a member?

There are two simple steps: fill in Membership Application Form and pay membership fees.

Fees are 140 zł for 6 months. Newcomers pay also 100 zł for 4 Toastmasters Manuals (it includes 2 basic and 2 advanced manuals).

4. I'm not sure if my English is good enough to participate in the meeting.

If you asked yourself about it your English is probably good enough :) The best way to make sure is to come to our meeting!

Moreover most of our members joined us because they wanted to improve their English. Why wouldn't you?

5. What are Toastmasters manuals?

Toastmasters program is about learning by doing. Nevertheless we do have handbooks to help us develop our communication, leadership and speaking skills and to learn in organized way.

6. What is the next step after joining the club?

Good question! It depends on your goals. The most important thing is that you will get help and support from every member in our club whatever you want to achieve. Do you have goals? If you don't it's going to change after you start coming to our meetings. First thing most members do - we choose our mentors who help us in the beginning of our journey.

7. I've got stage fright/i'm shy - is Toastmasters for me?

Definitely! Did you know that everyone has stage fright? Even professional speakers but good news is you can learn how to deal with it. Some of our members were shy in the beginning and most of our members used to hate speaking in public before they joined out club. It changes when you start beeing good at it :)

8. I think I don't need public speaking in my job or life.

Have you ever been silent for the whole day? Probably not. We speak every day and it depends on our communication skills if we have good relationships with our spouses, friends, bosses or clients.

Do you have any other questions? Feel free to contact me: membership@toastmasters-poznan.org
